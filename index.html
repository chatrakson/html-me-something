Traveling to Japan
My wife and I travel to Japan for our honeymoon Three facts about Japan:
  • The Japanese name for Japan is “Nihon” or “Nippon” which means “sun origin”.
  • Japan belongs to the continent of Asia. Japan is an island nation surrounded by the Sea of Japan to the East and the Pacific Ocean to the West.
  • The highest point in Japan in Mount Fuji, which stands at 3,776m (12,388ft).
Traveling to Italy
My wife and I travel to France with our friends Three facts about Italy:
  • The Italian flag is inspired by the French flag introduced during Napolean's 1797 invasion of the peninsula
  • The city of Naples gave birth to pizza
  • The average life expectancy at birth for an Italian is 79.54 years
Traveling to Croatia
My wife and I travel to Croatia because we liked Game of Throne Three facts about Croatia:
  • After years under different rules including the Kingdom of Yugoslavia, Croatia finally gained its full independence on June 25, 1991.
  • Croatia is also home to an original Egyptian sphinx. In 297, twelve sphinxes were brought to Split, Croatia. Currently, only a black granite one remains and is standing at the Peristyle.
  • Croatia may be one of Europe’s smaller country’s but it is actually the 127th largest country in the world.
